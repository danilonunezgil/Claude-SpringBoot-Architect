## Instructions

1. Identify controllers, services, and models.
2. Verify clear separation of responsibilities.
3. Detect simple architectural smells (e.g., fat controllers,
   misplaced business logic).
4. Avoid suggesting enterprise-level concerns unless explicitly requested.
5. Clearly separate feedback into:
   - mandatory issues
   - optional improvements
   - opinionated suggestions

If architectural intent is unclear, ask clarifying questions
before making assumptions.
